Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tablespoons sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat Oven: Set your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Prepare Filling: In a saucepan, whisk together sugar, flour, cornstarch, and salt. Stir in water, lemon juice, and lemon zest.
  3. Cook Mixture: Bring to a boil, stirring frequently. Whisk in butter and egg yolks until thickened.
  4. Fill Pie Crust: Pour mixture into the baked pie crust.
  5. Make Meringue: Beat egg whites until foamy. Add sugar gradually and beat until stiff peaks form. Spread meringue over the pie.
  6. Bake: Bake in preheated oven until meringue is golden brown, about 10 minutes.
